About Us

Colombo Branch

Our Colombo Branch is equipped with the latest medical technology and staffed by top specialists in various fields. Located in the heart of the city, it offers comprehensive medical services including emergency care, diagnostics, and specialized treatments.

Negambo Branch

Our Negambo Branch provides specialized care in orthopedics and rehabilitation. Located in the northern suburbs, it is easily accessible and offers a range of outpatient services, physical therapy, and orthopedic surgeries.

Galle Branch

The Galle Branch focuses on pediatric care and maternity services. It is located in a family-friendly neighborhood and provides a warm and welcoming environment for children and expectant mothers.
