Emergency Services

Our 24/7 Emergency Room provides immediate medical attention for acute illnesses, injuries, and life-threatening conditions. Specialized trauma care units treat severe injuries with advanced medical technology and highly trained staff.

Inpatient Services

Our inpatient services include general medicine, surgery, intensive care, maternity and neonatal care, pediatrics, and geriatrics. We provide comprehensive care for a variety of medical conditions, ensuring high-quality treatment and recovery support.

Outpatient Services

Our outpatient services offer general practitioner consultations, specialist consultations, and ambulatory surgery. We provide preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment of common and complex medical conditions.

Diagnostic Services

Our diagnostic services include radiology and laboratory services. We use advanced imaging techniques and conduct various tests to diagnose and monitor diseases, providing accurate and timely results to guide treatment plans.
